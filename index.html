What is a standard tip percentage?

In the United States, 15-20% is standard for sit-down restaurants. 18% is a common default. For counter service or takeout, 10-15% is typical. For exceptional service, 25% or more is generous. Tipping customs vary by country — freetipcalculator.app lets you pick any percentage from 0 to 100.

How do I split a restaurant bill evenly?

Use the split control on freetipcalculator.app to divide the total (bill plus tip) equally between 1 to 20 people. The per-person amount updates instantly as you adjust the number of people. This is the easiest way to figure out what each person owes at a group dinner.

How much should I tip for delivery?

For food delivery, 15-20% of the order total is standard. For large or complex orders, or deliveries in bad weather, consider tipping more. freetipcalculator.app works for any tipping scenario — restaurants, delivery, rideshare, salons, or any service.
